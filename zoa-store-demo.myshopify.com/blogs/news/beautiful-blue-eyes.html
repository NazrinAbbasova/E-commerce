in felix, landing, news, zoa blog
by ADMIN 1

April heralds the start of the festival season with Coachella kicking everything off this weekend! Here’s our favourite festivals, what we will be watching and more importantly... what we will be wearing!

Source: https://coinatmradar.com

Boho rules at Coachella, think floaty dresses, kimonos and fringing. Teoh and Lea's romantic mini dress is perfect for the dry desert heat, toughen it up with a safari jacket by Maison Scotch when the sun sets and the temperature drops. Accessories are everything at Coachella, complete the look with a wide brimmed hat, woven bucket bag and layers of jewellery.

LATITUDE FESTIVAL

12th – 15th July This countryside festival set in Henham Park, Suffolk, is truly a celebration of the arts. Since its conception in 2006, Latitude has consistently hosted huge names from the world of rock ‘n’ roll, alongside comedians, authors and artists of every discipline.
